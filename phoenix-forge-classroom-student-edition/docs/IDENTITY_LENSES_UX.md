# Wisp System Design: The Identity Lenses

Wisps are the ethereal patrons of childhood. In the Sovereign Archive architecture, Wisps act as **Identity Lenses**—visual mirrors that reflect the child's emerging identity threads.

## 1. The Magic of Reflection
We never label the child. Instead, the Wisps become more vibrant based on the **Chronicle**.
- **Vibrancy:** Completion of real-world activities related to a Wisp's domain increases its "glow" and complexity.
- **Closeness:** High frequency of interaction unlocks "Whisper Stories"—narratives where the Wisp reflects on the child's growth.

## 2. The Foundational Lenses

### MOSS (Nature & Discovery)
- **Lenses:** Naturalist, Observer, Protector, Explorer.
- **Grows via:** Nature Journal captures, outdoor trips, animal observations.
- **World Impact:** Blooms flowers and brings butterflies to Hearthhome.

### EMBER (Building & Physics)
- **Lenses:** Builder, Engineer, Inventor, Fixer.
- **Grows via:** Builder Sandbox projects, real-world LEGO/cardboard builds.
- **World Impact:** Spawns tools and sparks in the Workshop; stabilizes large structures.

### RIPPLE (Connection & Story)
- **Lenses:** Storyteller, Empath, Communicator, Helper.
- **Grows via:** Messenger Post missions, social check-ins, "Victory Journal" narrations.
- **World Impact:** Leaves ink-bubble trails; unlocks new pages in Memory Books.

### VOLT (Understanding & Logic)
- **Lenses:** Pattern-Finder, Logic-Master, Organizer, Strategist.
- **Grows via:** Clock Tower puzzles, pattern-matching, house organization missions.
- **World Impact:** Geometric light arrays; powers the Time Mirror and Memory Globe.

## 3. Identity Manifestation
When an **Identity Thread** reaches high confidence (e.g., "The Master Builder"), the corresponding Wisp (Ember) grants a **Wisp Gift**—a permanent, legendary artifact for the Museum that represents that specific part of the child's self.
