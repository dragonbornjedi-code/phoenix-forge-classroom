# Student Experience Boundary

Student Edition is the child-facing APK. It should translate the teacher framework into play.

## Show
- Short missions.
- Visual choices.
- Progress stars or version badges.
- Friendly retry prompts.
- Games, lessons, and build activities.
- Simple mood and energy check-ins.

## Do Not Show
- Adult analysis.
- Private teacher notes.
- Long explanations.
- Behavioral cue interpretation.
- Curriculum management tools.
- Diagnostic or clinical wording.

## Example Translations
- Teacher: Emotional regulation protocol.
- Student: Pause button game.

- Teacher: Cognitive abstraction mapping.
- Student: What job does this thing do?

- Teacher: Weekly progress audit.
- Student: Stars, badges, and "I got better at..." moments.

- Teacher: Failure post-mortem.
- Student: Oops, patch, retry.
