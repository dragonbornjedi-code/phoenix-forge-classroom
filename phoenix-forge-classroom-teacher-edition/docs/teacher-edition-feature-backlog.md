# Teacher Edition Feature Backlog

Adult-only features for planning, tracking, and adapting Ezra's homeschool experience.

## Curriculum Planner
- Seven-section curriculum library.
- Lesson-plan builder with age, energy, materials, duration, and setting.
- Activity variants for visual, physical, verbal, choice-based, and sensory methods.
- Teacher notes per activity.
- Link activities to Student Edition missions.

## Daily Dashboard
- Morning System Initialization.
- Energy, mood color, hunger, sleep, and transition notes.
- Today's missions.
- Recovery tools that worked.
- One-sentence daily observation.

## Progress Metrics
- Track concrete observations, not labels.
- Per-section weekly metrics.
- Skill streaks without shame mechanics.
- "Version check" history for comparing growth over time.
- Evidence attachments: photo, audio note, drawing, or build note.

## Behavioral Insight
- Cue library: yellow-state signs, red-state signs, overload signs, transition friction, fatigue signs.
- Context tags: time, sleep, food, noise, screen time, social load, task difficulty.
- Method library: co-regulation, movement, visual supports, timers, sensory supports, roleplay, reduced choices.
- Pattern review: "what usually works when this cue appears?"
- Repair review: what happened, what helped, what to try next.

## Lesson Adaptation
- Make easier: reduce steps, shorten duration, increase physical cues.
- Make harder: add a step, ask for prediction, require explanation, transfer to new object.
- Change method: visual to physical, verbal to roleplay, screen to hands-on.
- Pause lesson: body need, emotional reset, sensory reset, or transition support.

## Student Edition Publishing
- Select child-facing mission title.
- Select icon, badge, reward, and simple instructions.
- Hide teacher rationale and behavioral notes.
- Publish to Student Edition queue.
- Record completion and notes back to Teacher Edition.

## Safety And Privacy
- Teacher notes stay local and adult-only.
- Student sees only age-appropriate missions and rewards.
- Sensitive observations require explicit export.
- No public sharing by default.
