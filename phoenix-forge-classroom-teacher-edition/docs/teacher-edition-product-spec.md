# Teacher Edition: Childhood Development Navigation System

The Teacher Edition is the adult/admin interface for Phoenix Forge. It is not a management tool; it is a **Navigation System** designed to help parents consistently provide a rich, balanced childhood even in chaotic or unpredictable environments.

## 1. The Core Interface: Daily Expedition Board
The landing page of the app is the **Daily Expedition Board**. It elevates the traditional "Lesson Plan" into a living, intent-based navigation tool.

### Intent-Based Planning
- **The Priority Stack:** Draggable "Intent Tiles" instead of rigid time blocks.
- **Tile Expansion:** Tapping a tile reveals a deep "Knowledge Record" (Why it matters, alternative approaches, sensory accommodations, story hooks).
- **Historical Accuracy:** Completed items lock in their actual order, creating a truthful record of the day for the **Sovereign Archive**.

---

## 2. Childhood Compass (The CDNS Engine)
The system's highest responsibility is to ensure no area of development is neglected over months or years.

### Navigation Signals
- **Domain Health:** Visual indicators showing which developmental domains (Nature, Logic, Creation, Connection) are thriving and which "Need More Sunlight."
- **Intent Calibration:** The system suggests "Next Best Wins" based on recent gaps (e.g., "We haven't done fine motor work in two weeks; here are some fun options").

---

## 3. Narrative Wrapper Engine
Once the parent has accepted the day's intent, the system generates a cohesive story to unify the activities.
- **Function:** Transforms [Reading + Walk + Math] into [A Mission to Restore Signal Towers].
- **Outcome:** The child experiences a cohesive adventure; the parent executes a balanced developmental plan.

---

## 4. Archive Mentorship & Mentorship
- **MemoryEvent Approval:** Promoting child discoveries to the permanent **Living Chronicle**.
- **Parental Echoes:** Recording human context (voice/text) for the 20-year legacy.
- **Identity Thread Review:** Confirming long-term patterns discovered by the **Identity Formation Engine**.

---

## 5. Deployment Mandates
- **Mobile First:** Optimized for one-handed use on trails, in cars, or at campsites.
- **Offline Autonomy:** 100% functional without cloud dependencies.
- **Intent over Time:** Focused on *what* we are doing and *why*, not *when*.
