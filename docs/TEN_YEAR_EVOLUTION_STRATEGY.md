# PCAS: 10-Year Evolution Strategy

Phoenix Forge is built to evolve along with the child's neurological and social development.

## Stage 1: The Hearth (Ages 5-7)
- **Primary Interface:** Android APK (2D).
- **Narrative Depth:** First-person play, concrete actions, immediate feedback.
- **World State:** The Hearth-Cottage and immediate Garden.
- **Focus:** Discovery, sensory grounding, safety, and foundational literacy/math.

## Stage 2: The Village (Ages 8-10)
- **Primary Interface:** Android APK + PC Alpha.
- **Narrative Depth:** Third-person agency, community roles, "helping the village."
- **World State:** Hearthhome expands to include the **Market Square** and **Inventor Lab**.
- **Focus:** Skill acquisition, engineering, economic basics, and social-emotional collaboration.

## Stage 3: The Kingdom (Ages 11-13)
- **Primary Interface:** Godot RPG (3D).
- **Narrative Depth:** Complex branching stories, ethical choices, "Building a Legacy."
- **World State:** Multi-realm exploration. Portals to the **High Peaks** (Volt) and **Deep Oceans** (Ripple).
- **Focus:** Identity formation, advanced logic, abstract reasoning, and community contribution.

## Stage 4: The Universe (Ages 14-18)
- **Primary Interface:** Cross-Platform Master Suite.
- **Narrative Depth:** Self-authored quests, mentoring others, "The Master Forger."
- **World State:** Infinite procedural realms.
- **Focus:** Purpose, project-based mastery, mentorship, and life-path alignment.

## Continuity Mechanism
- **The Chronicle Mirror:** Every new version of the software begins by reading the **Living Chronicle** from the previous stage. 
- **The First Session:** Spark welcomes the user with a recap of their entire history ("Welcome back, Ezra. I remember when we found that blue feather together seven years ago...").
